81. Mutual Fund Tax in 2026: LTCG, STCG Explained Simply

Profit kiya ₹2 Lakh? Tax kitna dena hai? Ye rule 90% log nahi jante.

Mutual fund me 2 tarah ke tax hote hain. Galat samjha to CA ko extra fee deni padegi. 2026 ke naye rules ke hisaab se samjhte hain.

Equity Mutual Fund Tax

Holding Period Tax Type Rate
Less than 1 Year STCG 20%
More than 1 Year LTCG 12.5% above ₹1.25 Lakh profit

Example: ₹5 Lakh profit in 2 years. First ₹1.25 Lakh tax free. Baki ₹3.75 Lakh par 12.5% = ₹46,875 tax

Debt Mutual Fund Tax

Chahe 1 din rakho ya 10 saal. Saara profit aapke tax slab ke hisaab se tax hoga.

3 Important Points

  • Dividend: Ab dividend bhi aapke slab me add hoga. Tax free nahi hai
  • ELSS: 3 saal lockin ke baad bhi LTCG rule lagega
  • Tax Harvesting: Har saal ₹1.25 Lakh tak profit book karke tax bacha sakte ho

Final Thought: Tax se daro mat. Plan karke invest karo. Long term me 12.5% tax dena bhi sasta hai FD ke 30% tax se.

82. Emergency Fund: Kitna Hona Chahiye aur Kahan Rakhein?

Job gayi, hospital ka bill aaya, ghar me emergency. Tab kaam aata hai ye fund.

Emergency fund = Financial seatbelt. Ye nahi hai to 1 problem = loan + credit card debt. 2026 me har family ke paas ye hona hi chahiye.

Kitna Emergency Fund Chahiye?

Your Situation Months of Expense Example: ₹50K monthly expense
Salaried, Single 3-6 Months ₹1.5 Lakh to ₹3 Lakh
Salaried, Family 6-9 Months ₹3 Lakh to ₹4.5 Lakh
Business Owner / Freelancer 9-12 Months ₹4.5 Lakh to ₹6 Lakh

Rule: Monthly expense x Months = Your Emergency Fund

Emergency Fund Kahan Rakhein? 3 Rules

Rule 1: Liquid hona chahiye

2 din me nikal jaye. Stock ya FD todne me time na lage

Rule 2: Safe hona chahiye

Risk mat lo. Ye paise doobne nahi chahiye

Rule 3: Thoda return bhi mile

Bank me 3% se better option dekho

Best 4 Jagah 2026 Me

  1. Liquid Mutual Fund: 4-6% return. 1 din me paise. Tax bhi kam
  2. Sweep-in FD: Savings + FD dono ka fayda. Online tod sakte ho
  3. High Interest Savings Account: IDFC, AU Bank 6-7% dete hain
  4. Arbitrage Fund: 6-7% return. Tax equity jaisa 12.5% LTCG

3 Galti Jo Mat Karna

  • Stock me rakhna: Market 20% gir gaya to emergency me aur loss
  • Bilkul cash ghar me rakhna: Chori + Inflation kha jayega
  • Emergency fund ko trip ke liye use karna: Ye sirf asli emergency ke liye

Emergency Fund Kaise Banaye in 12 Months

Step Plan:

Target: ₹3 Lakh
SIP: ₹25,000/month in Liquid Fund
12 mahine me ban jayega. Bonus/Birthday money bhi daal do.

Final Thought: Pehle Emergency Fund, phir Investment. Bina seatbelt ke car mat chalao. 3-6 mahine ka kharcha side me rakho aur chain ki neend so jao.
