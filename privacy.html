Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24 July 2026

Unique Piece India upisteps.in par aapki privacy humare liye bahut important hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum aapka data kaise collect karte hain aur use kaise karte hain.

1. Hum Kaun Sa Data Collect Karte Hain

Jab aap hamari website visit karte hain to hum automatically ye information collect kar sakte hain:

  • IP Address aur Location
  • Browser Type aur Device Information
  • Pages Visited aur Time Spent
  • Cookies for better user experience

2. Data Ka Use Kaise Hota Hai

Hum aapka data use karte hain:

  • Website ko improve karne ke liye
  • Aapko relevant content dikhane ke liye
  • Security aur fraud prevention ke liye
  • Analytics ke liye jaise Google Analytics

3. Cookies

Humari website cookies use karti hai. Cookies choti files hoti hain jo aapke browser me store hoti hain. Aap chahe to browser settings se cookies ko disable kar sakte hain.

4. Third Party Links

Hamari website me dusri websites ke links ho sakte hain. Un websites ki privacy policy ke liye hum responsible nahi hain.

5. Data Security

Hum aapke data ko safe rakhne ke liye proper security measures use karte hain. Lekin 100% security ki guarantee nahi di ja sakti.

6. Contact Us

Agar aapke paas Privacy Policy ko lekar koi sawal hai to aap humse Contact page ke through contact kar sakte hain.

Note: Hum kabhi bhi bina bataye is Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain.
