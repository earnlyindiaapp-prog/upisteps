Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24 July 2026

Unique Piece India upisteps.in par aapki privacy humare liye sabse important hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ki hum aapka data kaise collect aur use karte hain.

1. Hum Kaun Sa Data Collect Karte Hain

Jab aap hamari website visit karte hain to hum ye information collect kar sakte hain:

  • IP Address aur Approximate Location
  • Browser Type, Device aur Operating System
  • Pages Visited, Time Spent aur Clicks
  • Cookies for better user experience aur analytics

2. Data Ka Use Kaise Hota Hai

Hum aapka data in kaamo ke liye use karte hain:

  • Website ko fast aur better banane ke liye
  • Aapko relevant content aur ads dikhane ke liye
  • Website traffic analyze karne ke liye jaise Google Analytics
  • Security aur fraud ko rokne ke liye

3. Cookies Policy

Humari website cookies use karti hai. Cookies choti text files hoti hain jo aapke browser me store hoti hain. Ye humein yaad rakhne me help karti hain. Aap chahe to browser settings se cookies ko block kar sakte hain.

4. Third Party Services

Hum Google Analytics, Ads aur dusri third party services use kar sakte hain. Unki apni privacy policy hoti hai. Unke liye hum responsible nahi hain.

5. Data Security

Hum aapke data ko safe rakhne ke liye industry standard security use karte hain. Lekin internet 100% secure nahi hota, isliye puri guarantee nahi de sakte.

6. Children's Privacy

Ye website 13 saal se kam umra ke bachcho ke liye nahi hai. Hum jaanbujhkar unka data collect nahi karte.

7. Policy Me Changes

Hum kabhi bhi bina notice ke is Privacy Policy ko update kar sakte hain. Latest version hamesha isi page par milega.

8. Contact Us

Privacy se related koi sawal ho to aap Contact page ke through humse baat kar sakte hain.
